## The Rock Johnson

<img src="/assets/images/therockheader.JPG" alt="" width="100%" class="rainbow-border">

This is The Rock. Probably the most wrestler of all time. Everyone knows who he is.

The Rock's real name is Dwajne Johnson and now he is an actor. He is most famous for his role of being a wave in Moana 2.

When he was a wrestler he won lots of championship belts and could do funny things with his eyebrows. This made him so popular his elbow was named the people's elbow. If you got hit with the people's (rock's) elbow it was almost certainly night night for you.

Here are some things he is famous for

- WWE Championship (8 times) 
- WCW Championship (2 times) 
- WWF Intercontinental Championship (2 times) 
- WWF Tag Team Championship (5 times) – with Mankind (3), The Undertaker (1), and Chris Jericho (1) 
- Royal Rumble 2000 winner
